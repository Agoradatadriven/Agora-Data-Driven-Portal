What happens when you hit create

  1. The form posts service_type + the tweaks (qty, platform) alongside the normal fields.
  2. add_task() creates the card exactly as it does today — nothing changes here.
  3. build_maintasks() reads the recipe and multiplies any per-unit step by its quantity (3 reels → Reel 1, 2, 3).
  4. set_task_maintasks() writes them onto the card in one go.
  5. Every sub-task carries its done test, so "finished" isn't an opinion.
  6. A revision attaches to one unit — and because the close guard already counts open sub-tasks, it blocks the card automatically.
