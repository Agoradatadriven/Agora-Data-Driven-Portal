## What Agora Is

Agora Data Driven is a Philippines-based, remote/offshore growth agency. At its core it
is a full-service digital marketing and data agency: it runs paid media, organic, email,
and creative like any strong marketing agency — and on top of that it builds the data
systems, automation, and machine learning that most agencies can't. Its positioning is a
data-driven growth system that connects performance marketing, creative, content
iteration, and market research into one weekly operating loop, turning business signals
into clear next actions instead of guesswork.

The founding belief: most businesses don't fail from lack of effort, they fail from lack
of clarity — running ads and producing content without a system to interpret results and
decide what to do next. Agora removes that waste with KPI systems, evidence-based
decisions, and a disciplined weekly cadence.

One-liner: "We don't just run ads. We run a KPI system that tells us what to do next
every week, with evidence — and we have the marketing, creative, and engineering teams
to execute it."

## The Most Important Thing for Scoring: Breadth Is an Advantage

Agora does a lot, and that width is a selling point, not a dilution. Read job posts
through this lens:

- A client asking for **only paid media / media buying** (Meta, Google, LinkedIn,
  TikTok, programmatic) is a **high fit**. They'll be glad to learn Agora also brings
  data, automation, and creative — Agora over-delivers relative to the ask.
- A client asking for **only automation / AI workflows** is a **high fit**. They'll be
  glad Agora also does marketing well.
- A client asking for **only creative, only email, only SEO, only a dashboard** is
  likewise a **high fit** — Agora has a dedicated team for each.

**Never penalize a job for being narrow.** A single-discipline marketing, creative,
data, or automation job with no red flags is a high score. The score comes down only
when a red flag is present, not when the ask is focused.

## Services / Offers

**Digital marketing (full-service).** Paid media and media buying across Meta, Google,
LinkedIn, TikTok, and programmatic (Trade Desk, DV360); organic social and SEO/content;
email marketing, lifecycle, and CRM (ActiveCampaign, HubSpot, Klaviyo-style flows,
nurture, newsletters); lead generation, funnels, and conversion systems.

**Creative & content production.** A dedicated creative team produces ad creative —
UGC-style video, statics, carousels, reels — plus scripts, hooks, copywriting (ad,
landing-page, newsletter, offer framing), branding, and website/landing-page design.
Comfortable with purely creative briefs and creative tied to performance. Creative
direction is driven by dashboard signals, run as a test → learn → scale-the-winner loop,
with reusable internal AI tools as accelerators and human creatives owning the final
output.

**Data-driven performance operating system (flagship).** Custom KPI dashboards that
track a business end-to-end and convert performance data into ranked weekly priorities.
Standard modules: Website Analytics, Meta/paid ads (with creative-level breakdowns),
Email, Sales (ecom/service/hybrid), Lead Magnet Performance, and an ML-enabled
Loyalty/Retention module. Every client runs a non-negotiable weekly cadence — data
refresh → mid-week optimizations → end-of-week learnings + next-week plan — with a
weekly KPI summary and 3–5 ranked actions.

**Automation & AI systems.** Marketing/workflow automation, data pipelines, and
AI/LLM-driven reporting, research, and content generation. Notably, Agora builds these
the proper way on scalable cloud infrastructure rather than brittle no-code tools.

**Market research (Industry Pulse Engine).** A scheduled research system that scrapes
and summarizes competitors, ad libraries, search results, and trends into a Market Pulse
Report with suggested creative/offer tests.

**Machine learning decision layer.** Predictive/recommendation layer on the dashboards —
likely-to-repurchase segments, next-best-offer, retention prediction — framed as
decision support, not modelling for its own sake.

**Also observed:** website design & development, chatbots, tracking/analytics
implementation, booking systems, Google Apps Script.

**Edge to emphasize:** Agora does AI automation and data engineering on GCP/BigQuery —
scalable, owned infrastructure — instead of no-code glue (Zapier/Make/n8n), and runs a
full marketing and creative operation. It fits jobs needing marketing, creative, data,
automation, or any combination.
