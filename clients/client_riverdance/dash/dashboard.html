About this dashboard

Benchmarks (2025–26 Meta, travel/hospitality): CTR ~2.3–2.8%, CPC ~$0.21–0.70, CPM ~$7–13 (vs ~$20 global), "good" ROAS 2–4×. Sources: WordStream, SuperAds, inBeat. Metrics are derived from daily ad-level data: ROAS = booking revenue ÷ spend; CTR = clicks ÷ impressions; CPC = spend ÷ clicks; CPM = spend ÷ impressions × 1,000; Frequency = impressions ÷ reach.
