What changed & why

  1. Bin and Activity moved to a bottom "System" group.
    They're audit/recovery utilities, not daily work — a trash can shouldn't sit above the Task Board. Utility items also render one step quieter (muted), so the rail's visual weight matches actual importance.
  2. Bin's badge goes quiet. purple 14 → muted 14
    Purple badges should mean "needs your attention" (pending access requests, awaiting approvals). A trash count is never actionable — loud styling on it trains everyone to ignore ALL badges.
  3. Mailboxes joins System.
    It's mail plumbing (connections), not people. "People & access" goes back to meaning exactly what it says: Accounts.
  4. Daily-use order: Clients → Task Board → Calendar.
    The first three items are now the three surfaces the team opens every day, in the order of a normal working session.
  5. Kept: group labels, icons, active-state style, the Task Board count, the account block + Sign out at the foot.
    The arrangement changes; the design language doesn't.
