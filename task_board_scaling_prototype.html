Task Board — scaling options

The board below is seeded with 45 services across 6 clients so the crowding is real. Toggle each option in the control bar and compare. Nothing here is wired to the backend — it's for choosing a direction.

1 Search

Filter by title, campaign or client as you type. The fastest way to find one service among many.

2 Density

Comfortable vs Compact cards. Compact drops the footer to fit 2–3× more per screen.

3 Cap columns

Show the first 8 per column + a “Show all”. No column grows unbounded; Closed starts collapsed.

4 Swimlanes

Group the board into per-client rows, each with its 4 mini stage columns. The long-term shape for many clients.

How to try it: type in search (e.g. reel or honey) — it filters both views. Flip Compact to see the density gain. Toggle Cap long columns to see In Process collapse to 8 with a “Show all”. Switch to Swimlanes to see the per-client grouping.

My recommendation: ship Search + Density + Cap first (all client-side, low-risk, fits the current filter bar), and adopt Swimlanes as a second view toggle if the flat board still feels crowded once you're at real volume.
